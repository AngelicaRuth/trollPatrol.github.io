Privacy Policy

How We Collect Information

We collect information to provide the Troll Patrol service: giving you power to make the Internet a human friendly place. We collect information from data your browser sends us. As most other sites, we also use "cookies" to understand how you use Troll Patrol, and to improve our service so it best fits your needs.

How We Use Information

Through the information you provide us, we are able to identify the areas of issue and address them. Furthermore, we communicate with you and about the things you care to know about. We care deeply about your privacy and anonmimity on the web, therefore we do not in any way share your data with anyone.

Changes To Policy

Troll Patrol is a project by 4 data geeks. We are always looking for new ways we can use the information you provide us to create greater value for you. This means our policy will undergo changes as we develop Troll Patrol. Information will be governed by the policy that is in place in the time of its collection.
